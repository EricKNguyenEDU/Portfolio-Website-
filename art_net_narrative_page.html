Net Art

I wanted to create a fun and simple narrative about a rat who wants to eat cheese and needs help from the viewer. I wanted the experience to feel like you were traveling with the rat on an adventure and that you were leading the way. My goal was to make the story interactive so the viewer feels personally involved in the rat’s journey. By combining playfulness with a sense of exploration, I hoped to make the narrative both engaging and memorable.
