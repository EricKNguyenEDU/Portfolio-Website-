What Do You MEME?

Memes and mashups in my piece were both fun and challenging to work with. At first, it was difficult to find the right memes, but once I did, they came together in a way that made sense. I think it’s interesting how different memes can still connect through similar meanings, especially when supported by text. My video is meant to represent what an average student experiences when they suddenly remember they have an assignment due the same day. Overall, the piece conveys the stress, urgency, and chaos of trying to finish and submit work on time.
