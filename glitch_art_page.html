Glitch Art

I use glitching in my images to represent a fractured perspective on life, showing how beauty and meaning can still emerge from distortion or imperfection. Through these works, I explore appreciation of life in different forms. The cherry blossoms symbolize new beginnings and the nature of existence, reminding us to cherish each moment. The evening sky highlights the overlooked beauty in nature, encouraging us to pause and admire it. And the image of men spending time together emphasizes the importance of valuing close relationships and treasuring the moments we share.
