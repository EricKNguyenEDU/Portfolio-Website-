3D & Animation

For this project, I decided to take a funny approach using deer and cars. I was inspired by a humorous video I found on YouTube that featured a goat-like character driving a car and chasing after a deer. My goal was to entertain my audience since I thought that animation was hilarious to watch. Overall, I don’t think the project was too overwhelming. Uploading and animating the work felt fairly easy to do. If I had the chance to go back and make some edits, I would add more detail by creating a larger environment. However, due to the limitations of my device, I wasn’t able to do that for this project.
