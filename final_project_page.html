Glitch Music video

For this project, I wanted to create a video that highlights an important topic. We all have one body and one mind, yet interestingly, we often create three different versions of ourselves. This idea inspired my project, which explores the three personalities people present in their daily lives. I based this concept on a Japanese philosophy that suggests individuals have three “faces”: one they show to strangers, one they show to those close to them, and a final face they reveal only to themselves, their truest self.
